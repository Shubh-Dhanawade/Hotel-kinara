About Hotel Kinara

Nestled in the serene and picturesque village of Tapola, Hotel Kinara offers a tranquil retreat away from the hustle and bustle of city life. Located in the beautiful hill station of Mahabaleshwar, our hotel combines natural beauty with comfortable accommodations to provide a unique and relaxing experience for our guests.
